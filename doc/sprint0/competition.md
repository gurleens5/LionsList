# LionsList vs Other Platforms

While other online marketplaces for buying and selling academic materials exist, LionsList offers unique functionality. Platforms commonly used by students include:

---

### [Kijiji](https://www.kijiji.ca/)

Kijiji is a general classifieds platform where users can buy and sell almost any item and service, including academic materials. However, it is not student-focused, and sellers often experience lowball offers, scams, and no-shows. Additionally, it lacks filtering by courses or university for academic materials. In contrast, LionsList is designed specifically for YorkU students, includes verified profiles, and is YorkU community-focused, removing the risks of a general marketplace.

---

### [Facebook Marketplace](https://www.facebook.com/marketplace/)

Facebook Marketplace is very similar to Kijiji, where anyone can buy and sell items, including academic materials. However, it is not exclusively built for verified YorkU students, increasing the risk of scams and unreliable buyers. Moreover, listings are organised by categories, with no option of academic materials or filtering by course codes or universities. LionsList differs by being student-exclusive and organized around YorkU courses, creating a trusted and more relevant marketplace for students.

---

### [York University Bookstore](https://campus.bookstore.yorku.ca/)

The official York University bookstore provides students with required course materials, but has limitations. Textbooks are generally sold at fixed, high retail prices and in new condition. Other materials in stock mostly reflect those currently in use, meaning older editions of textbooks or materials from previous semesters may be unavailable. Moreover, refunds, exchanges, and returns on eligible items are only allowed within short, specific date ranges, leaving students stuck with costly materials if they miss a deadline. On the contrary, LionsList gives students the flexibility of buying and reselling materials at any time and at their own prices, adapting to student demand without strict deadlines.

---

### [York University Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/yorku/)

Reddit is a forum-style platform where students often post academic materials they are looking to purchase or sell in the unofficial “yorku” subcommunity. While it helps students specifically reach the YorkU community, it is not a structured marketplace. Posts with listings often get buried and include anonymous users. Additionally, it is much harder to find buyers and sellers for upper-year or specialised classes, and there are no filters for course-specific listings. LionsList differs by focusing on listings within the York University community, increasing visibility and offering a searchable, constantly updated database.

---

## How Our Product Differs

In contrast to general marketplaces, the focused approach of LionsList makes buying and selling academic materials for students much easier. It is more suitable for those who are budget-conscious and on a time-crunch. The course-code filtering feature on the platform provides them with exactly what they need from verified sellers. On a general marketplace, students must search through countless irrelevant listings and face a higher risk of scams, and while convenient, the university bookstore charges full prices.  

For sellers who want to connect with serious buyers quickly, LionsList offers a simple listing process with a verified buyer base. Competing platforms attract a large user base, including lowballers and unreliable users, resulting in poor inquiries and unsold listings. LionsList is also advantageous for students with niche needs, who require rare or course-specific materials. It connects them with the entire YorkU community, including those who have previously taken similar courses and have exactly the materials they seek. In contrast, other marketplaces and the university bookstore may not carry specialised materials or may charge high prices with long delivery times. Moreover, online forum posts can easily get buried and cause students to face the same risks associated with general marketplaces.

---

## Why Competitors Overlook Specified Users

Competitors likely did not focus on these users because they prioritise scale and a general customer appeal for maximum profit. To adequately serve YorkU students, they would have to redesign their business model with the specific needs of YorkU students in mind. This would mean adding verification systems and course-code search filters, which require extra resources that are unnecessary for serving a much larger, general audience. Similarly, the campus bookstore would not benefit financially from selling academic materials for lower prices, as they need to account for operational costs as well as publisher pricing and licensing. They prioritise availability rather than affordability to ensure students receive the correct material necessary for their courses. As a result, YorkU students greatly benefit from LionsList, which offers reliable alternatives to overpriced or limited marketplace options.
