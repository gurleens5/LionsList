# Project Objectives

The primary objective of LionsList is to provide a secure and trusted environment where York University students can buy and sell academic materials, such as textbooks, personal notes, lab kits, stationery, and study guides. To aid with this, the platform requires YorkU email authentication upon sign-up, reducing the risk of scams and preventing access by external users. Moreover, LionsList aims to reduce the costs of academic materials for students by making them more accessible and affordable. Unlike general marketplaces, it serves as a centralized platform exclusively for the York University community. It is designed to be user-friendly and reliable, aiming to connect buyers and sellers efficiently while promoting transparency.

---

# Key Users

**Bella** is a first-year biology student who needs affordable lab kits, including a lab coat and goggles, for her required labs as well as some textbooks. She values affordability, convenience, and trusted sellers. Her goal is to reliably find the exact materials she needs for her courses quickly and confidently.

**Jimmy** is a fifth-year mathematics student who often purchases and sells his textbooks from his previous courses to earn some extra money. He values efficiency and simplicity, and likes a quick listing process on a trusted platform. He also likes to be assured that he will deal with serious and legitimate buyers from York University to avoid being scammed.

---

# Key Scenarios

**Bella** has just enrolled late in a new mathematics course with limited seats. She realizes that the course requires a textbook for weekly homework assignments, with the first one due in two days. The textbook costs over $150 at the campus bookstore, which she absolutely cannot afford. She spends hours searching online marketplaces, but only finds incorrect editions or overpriced copies. Upon finding an affordably priced used textbook, she messages the seller, only to wait hours for a reply. She discovers that the textbook had already been sold, and the listing wasn’t updated by the seller. Frustrated and running out of time, Bella needs a reliable way to find the correct textbook as soon as possible at an affordable price.

**Jimmy** is about to graduate in two months and needs to get rid of three of his mathematics textbooks from last semester. He decides to make a listing on Kijiji, but only receives responses from known scammers or people lowballing him with offers much below his listed price. Eventually, he receives a seemingly authentic message from a buyer who agrees to purchase all of the textbooks, and they arrange a meeting. The buyer never shows up and deletes their account, leaving Jimmy frustrated with his books unsold and his time wasted. He requires a reliable platform where he can confidently sell his academic materials to verified buyers, preventing him from being scammed.

**Jimmy** is working on a final project for his advanced mathematics course and wants to reference proofs from an out-of-print monograph widely used by students in past semesters. It is unavailable at the campus bookstore and on major online marketplaces. He searches for several days and eventually finds a listing on a foreign website for a used copy, but it is too expensive and would be delivered long after his project’s deadline. He also reaches out to current students in his program and alumni, but none have a copy. He hopes that making a post on the university student forum may help, but it gets buried and goes unanswered. Jimmy requires a platform where he can quickly search for academic materials used by other York students for the same courses, increasing the chances of finding the exact resources he needs in time.

---

# Key Principles

1. The design should prioritise creating a secure and trustworthy environment for all YorkU students, even if it means a longer sign-up process with extra verification steps.

2. The design should allow students to quickly buy and sell academic materials for their specific courses, even if it means simplifying or excluding advanced features.
